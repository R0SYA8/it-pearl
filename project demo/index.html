The Outdoor Experience Alliance

Camping Trips

Camping trips are offered for all age groups and range from one night to a week. Gender-specific trips are available, or sign up with your kids for an adventerous family vacation. Whatever your ideal trip looks like, experience the freedom of sleeping under the stars.


Trail Cleanups

Join us in experiencing the joy of getting dirty and giving back. Trail cleanup events seek to rediscover the natural beauty of our state parks while creating bonds between members of the community. Grab a few friends, put on your oldest clothes, and sign up for an upcoming cleanup day.


Internships

Does the thought of spending your summer leading adventurous outdoor trips excite you? Consider joining our team as an intern. You'll develop leadership skills, be a role model to the children of our community, and have a lot of laughs with our fun-loving staff. Anyone can apply!



At OEA, we believe that time spent outdoors is more than just relaxing. We have seen firsthand the confidence-inspring power of the outdoors for those who take the time enjoy it. Regular exposure to activities outdoors has been shown to impact behavior and moods of both the young and young-at-heart. Aside from relieving stress, time spent outside has the potential to lead to improved mental health and clarity, a stronger immune system, and better vision.



Alliance Membership

OEA is funded entirely by donations and proceeds from our volunteer-operated outdoor equipment store. Make a donation or become an Alliance volunteer.
